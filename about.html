About BBHS Café

BBHS Café is a student-run café located in a dedicated café area near the PE shelter, with food preparation and operations based inside the K1 room.

It is run by students from mixed year groups, giving them real-world experience in hospitality, teamwork, customer service, and cash handling.

The café focuses mainly on hot and cold drinks along with light breakfast items, providing students with a quick and enjoyable service before school.
